I build the plumbing of capital markets.

I am Chief Executive of the Capital Markets Solutions business at Contemi, where we operate Nova CMX — a multi-asset, multi-market post-trade platform used by Tier-I sell-side brokers, bank brokerages, and investment banks across South-East Asia, Australia and New Zealand, the UK, and Europe.

My work is unglamorous by design: clearing, settlement, risk control, compliance, resilience. The systems no one sees until they stop working. I try to make them quieter, faster, and harder to break.

Now

What I’m working on, this quarter.

  • Platform Evolving Nova CMX from a post-trade platform into a full capital-markets operating system — one ledger, one rules engine, one view of risk across cash equities, derivatives, and digital assets.
